It is a smartphone app which provides on-demand delivery service to users. It connects available persons who are willing to deliver goods to others who need goods delivered. Runners use their own transportation on their own time and Getgo charges 20% of the delivery fee. The total process is very simple, registered Getgo users asks for a Delivery using the Getgo app, a Getgo runner is then dispatched to the pickup location then securely brings the package to the drop off location. Once approve by the requester, The payment is immediately transferred to the runner The requester`s credit/debit card is used as the sole payment method
